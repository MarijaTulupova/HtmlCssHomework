About Us

Pastalicious was born out of a deep passion for Italian culture, cuisine, and the timeless tradition of gathering around the table to share a meal. Founded by Chef Massimo, who grew up surrounded by the rich culinary heritage of a small Italian village, our restaurant brings the flavors and stories of Italy to life in every dish. Chef Massimo learned the art of Italian cooking from his grandmother, who passed down family recipes that had been perfected over generations. Every sauce is simmered with patience, every pasta is rolled by hand, and every dish is crafted with the love and authenticity that make Italian food so special. Whether you're enjoying a cozy dinner for two or a festive family gathering, Pastalicious is our way of sharing a slice of Italy with each of our guests.

Our chefs

Massimo Bottura

Massimo Bottura is an Italian restaurateur and the chef patron of Osteria Francescana, a three-Michelin-star restaurant based in Modena, Italy. It has been ranked No.1 on The World's 50 Best Restaurants list twice and is currently part of the Best of the Best list.

Salvatore Cuomo

Salvatore Shigeta is an Italian-born Japanese celebrity chef, restaurateur and media personality, known for his food-focused television shows and cookbooks, and his restaurants across Asia. He is known in Japan as "The Pizza Man" and "The Grand Chef".

Cristina Bowerman

Cristina Bowerman is an Italian chef, who holds a Michelin star at Glass Hostaria in Rome. She studied foreign languages and law, and worked as a graphic designer. However, she trained at Le Cordon Bleu and began working as a chef when she returned to Italy.
