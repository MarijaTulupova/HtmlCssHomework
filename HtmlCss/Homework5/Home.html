Bean There, Brew That

At Bean There, Brew That, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it is an experience, a ritual, and a moment to savor. Nestled in the heart of Gevgelija,Macedonia , our cozy shop combines the warmth of a traditional café with a modern twist. Step inside, and you'll be greeted by the comforting aroma of freshly ground beans, the soft hum of conversation, and the sight of our baristas crafting each cup with care.

Our passion for quality shines through in everything we do. We proudly brew our coffee using locally-sourced, fair-trade beans, ensuring that each cup not only tastes exceptional but also supports sustainable practices. From espresso to pour-overs, every drink is made to order, capturing the full spectrum of flavors that make coffee so captivating. We also offer a selection of teas, house-made syrups, and dairy-free options, so everyone can find their perfect drink.

Whether you're here to relax, catch up on work, or spend time with friends, our shop provides a welcoming space where you can feel at ease. Soft lighting, comfortable seating, and a collection of local artwork create a space that feels like a second home. Our shelves are stocked with books and games to enjoy, and our free Wi-Fi makes it easy to stay connected.

And if you are in a hurry? No problem! We offer convenient takeout options so you can enjoy your favorite drink on the go. Come visit us at Bean There, Brew That, where every cup is crafted with care, and every visit feels like coming home.

What are we known for?

Seasonal Specials – Our menu changes with the seasons! From the spicy warmth of our Pumpkin Spice Latte in the fall to the refreshing Iced Mint Mojito in the summer, we bring you new and exciting flavors all year long.
Single-Origin Brews – We are passionate about our coffee’s journey. Our rotating selection of single-origin beans showcases unique flavors from around the world, with each cup telling a story of the farm it came from.
Handcrafted Pastries and Treats – Our pastries are baked fresh every morning by local bakers and pair perfectly with any drink. Try our buttery croissants, waffles, cheesecake etc.
Brewing Workshops – Want to brew the perfect cup at home? Join one of our workshops and learn from our baristas as they share techniques for pour-over, French press, and more.
Art and Music Nights – We believe in supporting local artists. Stop by for our monthly art exhibit featuring local painters and photographers, or join us for live music every Friday night!
