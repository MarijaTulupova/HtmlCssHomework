Our Story

Bean There, Brew That was born from a love for coffee and a desire to create a space where people could come together, share stories, and slow down in today’s fast-paced world. It all began in 1995, when Giuseppe, a longtime coffee enthusiast, decided to turn their passion for brewing the perfect cup into something more meaningful. After years of traveling and tasting coffee from around the world, Giuseppe dreamt of opening a coffee shop that would not only serve exceptional drinks but also foster a sense of community and connection.

We started with a small but cozy space in the heart of Gevgelija,Macedonia, with a simple goal: to make every cup of coffee an experience. From the very beginning, we’ve been committed to quality, sourcing our beans from local roasters who prioritize fair trade practices and sustainable farming. Our dedication to supporting ethical producers is at the heart of what we do, ensuring that every sip you take is both delicious and responsibly sourced.

Our Mission

At Bean There, Brew That, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a moment of connection, creativity, and comfort. Our mission is to serve not only the finest coffee but also to create an inviting space where everyone feels welcome, valued, and inspired. Whether you're here for a morning pick-me-up, a quiet afternoon to work, or a place to gather with friends, we strive to offer an experience that enriches your day and supports the local community.

We’re proud to offer a menu that celebrates the diversity of coffee culture—from expertly brewed single-origin espressos to unique seasonal creations. Our goal is to bring you something new and exciting with every visit, whether that’s a bold new brew or a cozy new atmosphere.

Meet the Team

We’re a small but passionate team of coffee lovers, baristas, and creatives who all share a deep commitment to delivering the best experience for our customers. Our baristas are trained not just in brewing techniques but also in the art of conversation and hospitality. From the moment you walk through our door, you’ll be greeted by friendly faces eager to share their knowledge of coffee and offer personalized recommendations based on your taste.

Behind the scenes, we have a dedicated team that works tirelessly to bring you freshly baked pastries, seasonal treats, and special events. Our chefs, bakers, and event coordinators pour their creativity and passion into everything they do, from crafting our unique menu items to organizing fun community gatherings.

Together, we are more than just a coffee shop—we are a family dedicated to serving you, building lasting relationships, and creating a space that feels like home.
